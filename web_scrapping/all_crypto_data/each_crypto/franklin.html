Franklin Coin Price & Market Data

Franklin price today is $0.023592354951 with a 24-hour trading volume of $837,973. FLY price is up 6.1% in the last 24 hours. It has a circulating supply of 330 Million FLY coins and a total supply of 1.7 Billion. If you are looking to buy or sell Franklin, Gate.io is currently the most active exchange.

The Fly token was designed to provide customers with an opportunity for discounts and additional benefits from the VRM and Black Ocean ecosystems.

VRM is a proprietary HFT company founded in August 2018, it focuses on trading AI-based quantitative HF strategies on the crypto market and provide MM solutions to digital asset exchanges. The company works on all major trading venues, such as BitMEX, Bitflyer, OKEx, Huobi, Binance, Derebit, Bit.com, Bithumb, Liquid, BTSE with a market share ranging from 6.2% to 49% each, their daily average trading volume is around 15-20 Billion USD. In addition, VRM has multiple businesses in its ecosystem including VRM Research, VRM Capital Management, VRM Mining and etc.

Black Ocean is a liquidity-provision platform for institutional customers and crypto trading venues.
The liquidity pool provides retail-oriented trading venues with liquidity and order execution.
The dark pool provides institutional users with a trading venue for the execution of large orders exceeding 5 BTC/300k USD without a counter-party risk and influence on the market.
