It has a circulating supply of 93 Million STEP coins and a total supply of 630 Million. If you are looking to buy or sell Step Finance, FTX is currently the most active exchange.

What is Step Finance?

Step Finance is a platform that acts as the front page of Solana. Users of the platform will be able to visualize, analyze, execute, and aggregate transactions across all contracts and products on the Solana ecosystem such as Serum, Raydium, Mercurial, and more in a single page. Portfolio can be monitored in which users can manage their wallets, LP positions, yield farms, and margin positions.

Users of Step Finance can also execute Swaps and enter various yield farms and automated vaults from within Step with any fees on such value accrual services going back to STEP stakers.

What is the STEP token economics?

Max Supply: 1,000,000,000

Emission Period: 2 years

Weekly Emission Reduction: -4%

Founder Lockup: Lockup over 2 years vested in 25% increments every 6 months.

Presale Lockup: 2 years vesting. 50% available after 1 year, 50% available year 2.
