Iridium (IRD) Prospectus:

Community Focused:
It is our desire to grow the Iridium (IRD) community to be one of the best in the world. Without a strong community you can't build trust. Without trust, you can't have a successful cryptocurrency.  We are a community driven project where every community members voice counts, you talk, we listen!

Privacy and Anonymity:
By default with Iridium (IRD), your transactions are decentralized, completely anonymous, secure, 100% untraceable and your wallet balance remains private forever.  There is no “peeking in” other Iridium (IRD) wallets like with bitcoin and other cryptocurrencies.  Your privacy matters to us!

By using Iridium (IRD) not only are you safe, you... “KEEP YOUR BUSINESS YOUR OWN!”

Untraceable transactions are achieved using the technology in CryptoNote called ring signatures.  Ring signature verification requires all transactions to be signed on behalf of the group to which the individuals belong. Making the verification process impossible to trace back to the original creator, since all the signatures of the members of the group are indistinguishable from each other.

Capped Supply:
Iridium (IRD) is an Egalitarian Proof-Of-Work Cryptocurrency with the maximum amount of coins capped at 25 million.  Just like the precious metal, Iridium (IRD) is rare, scarce and a true store of value and we plan on keeping it that way.  Unlike the worlds reserve banks, we will never mint more!

No Premine:
With Iridium (IRD) there was no ICO, pre-mine, token distribution, or any other mechanism for giving an unfair advantage to the ""founders"" of the cryptocurrency.  This makes Iridium (IRD) one of the few “Fair Cryptocurrencies” in the market place today!

Iridium Features:
- Decentralized, Anonymous, Secure, Untraceable, Rare, Cryptocurrency and true “Store Of Value”
- Stable, Global Distribution network backed by one of the largest ISP providers in the game
- Fully E-commerce Enabled
- Capable of being used on over 50 million websites via WooCommerce
- Dedicated Development Team, fully versed in crypto, E-commerce and big-data.
- Enthusiastic Community - Well maintained Discord and Telegram groups

Use Cases:
Iridium (IRD) has a fairly simple set of use cases compared to the many other crypto currencies out on the market today. There are 4 problems that we look to solve with Iridium (IRD).

Fair-Mining Investment:
Looking for a high-reward opportunity within a small market cap coin.  Want to use some of your spare GPU/CPU mining power on a coin that still has good returns.  Want to mine for a coin that provides a fair-mining opportunity and doesn’t let giant miners push out the small ones.  Iridium (IRD) is an Egalitarian Proof-Of-Work Cryptocurrency, which means we ensure equal opportunity for all miners and treat them fairly.  You will not find ASIC miners here, Iridium (IRD) is decentralized!

Commerce:
You need a way to accept crypto as a payment method on your WordPress or E-commerce store. You need to use a coin with committed and focused development towards an E-commerce solution. You’re not into the idea of centralized payment networks and want to keep support for a coin that is truly anonymous. You’re looking for a coin that has a stable global set of nodes.  Iridium (IRD) is for you!

Anonymous Transactions:
You are looking for a safe and easy way to transfer value from one person to another. You want to make sure that you have the right support if something goes wrong. You are looking for a coin that is a 100% decentralized, anonymous, secure, untraceable, rare and true “Store Of Value” Iridium (IRD) coin has exactly what you are looking for.

We keep you safe, you... “KEEP YOUR BUSINESS YOUR OWN!”

Extensibility and Involvement:
You have skills and talents in development, documentation, writing or design and you’d like to contribute to a community that needs them. You want to support a distributed development concept. Iridium (IRD) provides a lot of mechanisms for integration with outside technologies. We have a dedicated integration team creating API’s for public consumption and integration into third-party products. Our dev team brings over 50 years of experience in B2C and B2B technologies.  Want to get involved in a community project that really cares?  Come join us, Iridium (IRD) is a blockchain that has set its sights on the real world.  Iridium (IRD), People Are Power, putting community first!
