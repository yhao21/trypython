Hathor Coin Price & Market Data

Hathor price today is $0.671191 with a 24-hour trading volume of $2,422,948. HTR price is down -6.6% in the last 24 hours. It has a circulating supply of 220 Million HTR coins and a total supply of 869 Million. If you are looking to buy or sell Hathor, KuCoin is currently the most active exchange.

Hathor's technology is a natural evolution of Bitcoin's blockchain. Instead of arranging the transactions in a Merkle Tree inside the blocks, we arrange the transactions in a DAG - outside the blocks - which are confirmed by the blocks. This novel design allows Hathor to be highly scalable AND decentralized.
Hathor also tackles the complexity of creating a new token. It's easy and fast. There are no such things as ""gas,"" ""solidity,"" and all “ERC 20"" paraphernalia. Tokens in the network operate with the same scalability and security parameters as the native HTR token.
Hathor's architecture is based on the Ph.D. thesis of Hathor's founder Marcelo Brogliato, and it is a completely novel architecture.
