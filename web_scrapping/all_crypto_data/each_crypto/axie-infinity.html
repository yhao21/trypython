Axie Infinity Coin Price & Market Data

Axie Infinity price today is $128.62 with a 24-hour trading volume of $1,139,616,296. AXS price is up 6.2% in the last 24 hours. It has a circulating supply of 61 Million AXS coins and a total supply of 270 Million. If you are looking to buy or sell Axie Infinity, Binance is currently the most active exchange.

AXS is the governance token for the Axie Infinity game. Token holders will be able to shape and vote for the direction of the game universe. This is unlike traditional games where all decisions are made by the game developers. AXS holders will be able to stake their tokens to earn more AXS and even vote for governance proposals.

The other token in the Axie universe is Smooth Love Potion (SLP). SLP is used for the purpose of breeding and can be earned by winning battles and adventures in the game. It is not a governance token, therefore it is not used for voting.
