What exchange is Audius on?

Traders can buy or sell Audius (AUDIO) on top exchanges such as Binance, Crypto.com, Gate, Uniswap, and FTX.

What is Audius?

Audius is a decentralized music streaming protocol. Artists can upload their music to the network which will be streamed by nodes to listeners through a mobile app. AUDIO is the native token for the platform and is used for staking, governance, and incentivized earnings for artists, fans, and node operators. The Audius project recently announced a partnership with Tik Tok in which musics on the platform will be available to users on one of the largest social network in the world.
