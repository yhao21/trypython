Rubic Coin Price & Market Data

Rubic price today is $0.281733 with a 24-hour trading volume of $1,590,769. RBC price is up 20.4% in the last 24 hours. It has a circulating supply of 110 Million RBC coins and a total supply of 124 Million. If you are looking to buy or sell Rubic, Uniswap (v2) is currently the most active exchange.

Rubic is a multichain DEX aggregator, with instant & cross-chain swaps for Ethereum, BSC, Polygon, Harmony, Tron & xDai, limit orders, fiat on-ramps and more! The aim of the project is to deliver a complete ONE-STOP FULL-CIRCLE decentralized trading platform. Rubic.exchange is a place where users can complete cross-chain swaps using different solutions to get the best rates from all leading trading protocols.

Who created Rubic?
Rubic's team has been present in the crypto sphere since 2017. First their product was platform MyWish -the leading platform for Smart contracts builders for blockchains (NEO, Ethereum, TRON, EOS, RSK, and WAVES) supported by TRON, Binance, Neo, OKEX, after that It has delivered several successful projects by now and has worked with big players such as Binance, Tron, NEO, and others. Rubic's platform was launched in September 2020.

What is the purpose of RBC?
Rubic platform has an ERC-20 token, «RBC», which is used as the fuel within the Platform.

Where can I learn more about Rubic (RBC)?
You can find information about Rubic and RBC here: https://rubic.finance
