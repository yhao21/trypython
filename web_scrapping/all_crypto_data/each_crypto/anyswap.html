Anyswap Coin Price & Market Data

Anyswap price today is $11.30 with a 24-hour trading volume of $18,689,255. ANY price is up 2.2% in the last 24 hours. It has a circulating supply of 17 Million ANY coins and a total supply of 100 Million. If you are looking to buy or sell Anyswap, BitMart is currently the most active exchange.

Anyswap is a fully decentralized cross chain swap protocol, based on Fusion DCRM technology, with automated pricing and liquidity system. Anyswap enables swaps between any coins on any blockchain which uses ECDSA or EdDSA as signature algorithm, including BTC, ETH, USDT, XRP, LTC, FSN, etc.
