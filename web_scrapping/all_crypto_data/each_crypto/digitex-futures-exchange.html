Digitex Token Coin Price & Market Data

Digitex Token price today is $0.002916500489 with a 24-hour trading volume of $386,734. DGTX price is down -1.9% in the last 24 hours. It has a circulating supply of 850 Million DGTX coins and a total supply of 1 Billion. If you are looking to buy or sell Digitex Token, KuCoin is currently the most active exchange.

Digitex.io is a commission-free cryptocurrency futures and Spot Market exchange with a rapid-fire one-click trading ladder and high leverage. Traders can buy and sell perpetual swap futures contracts on Bitcoin or Ethereum against the US Dollar and pay no transaction fees on any trades. As well as traders will not pay any taker or maker fees on Spot Market.

Commission-free trading is made possible by using its own cryptocurrency, called the DGTX token, as the native currency of the futures exchange. Instead of covering costs by charging transaction fees on trades, Digitex meets the operational costs of running the futures exchange by creating and selling a small number of new DGTX tokens each year.
