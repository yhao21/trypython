Zeitcoin Price & Market Data

Zeitcoin price today is $0.000037324572 with a 24-hour trading volume of $0.004276908896. ZEIT price is down -0.3% in the last 24 hours. It has a circulating supply of 37 Billion ZEIT coins and a total supply of 99 Billion. If you are looking to buy or sell Zeitcoin, YoBit is currently the most active exchange.

Zeitcoin is a Scrypt based PoW/PoS hybrid coin. It is created in homage to the Zeitgeist Movement.

"None are more hopelessly enslaved
than those who falsely believe they are free."

The objective of ZEIT
- A new Proof of Work / Proof of Stake resource with a purpose.
- To spread the word of the teachings of the Zeitgeist Movement.
- To be used as a complementary currency involved in trading for resources.
- 99,000,000,000 total ZEIT will be made, enough to involve the longevity of this revolutionary concept.
