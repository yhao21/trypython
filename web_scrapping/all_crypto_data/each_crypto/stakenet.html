Stakenet Coin Price & Market Data

Stakenet price today is $0.158968 with a 24-hour trading volume of $10,252.88. XSN price is down -1.1% in the last 24 hours. It has a circulating supply of 120 Million XSN coins and a total supply of 124 Million. If you are looking to buy or sell Stakenet, WhiteBIT is currently the most active exchange.

Stakenet is a Lightning Network-ready open-source platform for decentralized applications (dApps) with its native cryptocurrency – XSN. It is the first blockchain with Masternodes to be Lightning Network ready. XSN has instant, virtually feeless, and unlimited transactions per second, and has the first Lightning Network DEX running on its network. XSN is the first, next to Bitcoin and Litecoin, to ever perform Lightning Swaps.

XSN is digital money, like bitcoin in the Bitcoin blockchain, or ether in Ethereum, as its supply is not controlled by any government or company. With Stakenet’s Trustless Proof of Stake, users can stake their coins from cold storage (such as a hardware wallet) and receive staking rewards whilst their funds are offline and not at risk - with the goal of offering strong security
