About the DogData BONE tokens

What is ShibNFT ?

DogData is a community driven DeFi protocol offering trading in Dog Tokens and creation of a certificated NFT for your real life Dog 

On ShibNFT.com you are able too yield farm the Dog Token pools by owning the Dog Breed SuperDoge. Furthermore, due to the decentralized nature of being an AMM (Automated Market Maker), ShibNFT pays 80% of trading fees back too the Dog Token owners that trade , 20% of the trading fees go too the unique SuperDoge NFT owner.

ShibNFT is designed too be the most interesting and useful NFT Web3 environment

What is the BONE token?

BONE is the DogData ecosystem token  ;

A Payment token for sale of a real life pet Dog using  DogData NFT  

Pool token for the Dog token Swap

Dog products and Dog Food market discount token

What is the ShibNFT ?

A Ethereum based market for Dog sales and a marketplace for crypto related unique  Doge NFTs designed too harness the potential of wrapping other blockchains Binance an Ethereum Cash into both Dog sales and Dog token trading 

The History of DogData

DogData team is a mix of Crypto and Web3 enthusiasts historically involved in finance, marketing and the worlds Dog breeding and international DogShows. 

MyDogData.com Quickly gathered over 100,000 registered users, It was an exciting platform idea that spoke to the ethos of true decentralization….with the possibility of using a NFT as a solution for certificating Dog data and Dog ownership 

Due to the 2021 explosion of Defi and the influx of popular meme Dog tokens the team began developing  a automated market making swap protocol anticipated due for launch tok thr DogData community in August 2021

BONE token listings 

The token was first listed on Hotbit in Summer 2020 and after having immediate appeal with the crypto community , as the first Dog project offering too use blockchain and NFT for Dog life data. 

  • Listed on Uniswap March 2021 
  • Listing on BiBox June 2021
  • Listing on Exmo July 2021
