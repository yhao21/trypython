Earthcoin Price & Market Data

Earthcoin price today is $0.002876770605 with a 24-hour trading volume of $575,018. EAC price is up 2.2% in the last 24 hours. It has a circulating supply of 13 Billion EAC coins and a total supply of 13.5 Billion. If you are looking to buy or sell Earthcoin, AEX is currently the most active exchange.

Earthcoin is a descendant of Litecoin. Earthcoin uses a 365 day period and its block reward varies in a sine curve with an amplitude of 2,000 over a period of one year (like the Earth moving around the Sun).

Earthcoin block reward starts at 10,000 coins and adjusts at each block. A maximum of 12,000 coins per block will be reached after about 3 months from launch, then descend gradually to approximately 10,000 coins per block at about 6 months. Then a new minimum of 8,000 coins per block will be reached at about 9 months and this figure goes back up again to 10,000 coins per block after one year. After this, the payout is cut in half.
