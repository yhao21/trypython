SynchroLife Coin Price & Market Data

SynchroLife price today is $0.011521907821 with a 24-hour trading volume of $161.50. It has a circulating supply of 66 Million SYC coins and a total supply of 100 Million. If you are looking to buy or sell SynchroLife, LATOKEN is currently the most active exchange.

SynchroLife is an artificial intelligence based social restaurant search platform with token rewards for restaurant reviews and photos. SynchroLife is building the world's most intelligent and trustworthy restaurant discovery platform with artificial intelligence and blockchain technology. Through artificial intelligence, the platform learns the tastes of each user based on their actions and is able to provide users with personalized search results and recommendations. With the SynchroCoin token, the platform connects both restaurants and diners together by allowing users to earn tokens for their content and reviews as well as earn tokens cashback when dining at participating restaurants. Users can also use the tokens to buy digital coupons and pay for meals at restaurants.
