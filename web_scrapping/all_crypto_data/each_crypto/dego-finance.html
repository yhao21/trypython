Dego Finance Coin Price & Market Data

Dego Finance price today is $8.16 with a 24-hour trading volume of $9,531,993. DEGO price is up 1.2% in the last 24 hours. It has a circulating supply of 5.4 Million DEGO coins and a total supply of 9.84 Million. If you are looking to buy or sell Dego Finance, Gate.io is currently the most active exchange.

Dego.finance创造了跨链的NFT + DeFi协议和基建设施，致力探索 NFT 的金融功能性的落地，包括 NFT 的创建、交易、拍卖与 DeFi 应用。
DEGO 提供上层建筑赋能区块链项目、支持用户获取，发行定制 NFT 与 NFT挖矿等场景。DEGO正在通过Substrate搭建平行链，以实现NFT资产跨链、整合应用程序和底层协议。

Dego.finance created a cross-chain NFT+DeFi Protocol & Infrastructure, and an open NFT ecosystem where anyone can mint NFTs, initiate NFT mining, auctions and trading.

Dego.finance also offers a cross-chain infrastructure to enable Blockchain projects to grow user base, distribute tokens, and develop diverse applications. DEGO is building a Parachain through Substrate, enabling cross-chain NFT asset transfers, combining applications and underlying protocols.
