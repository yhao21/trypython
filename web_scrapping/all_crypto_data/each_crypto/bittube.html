BitTube Coin Price & Market Data

BitTube price today is $0.005502813763 with a 24-hour trading volume of $10,666.98. TUBE price is up 12.2% in the last 24 hours. It has a circulating supply of 320 Million TUBE coins and a total supply of 34.1 Million. If you are looking to buy or sell BitTube, Bittrex is currently the most active exchange.

BitTube as a company offers several products that make up an ecosystem. At the heart of this ecosystem is a blockchain-based technology called AirTime, an ad-free monetisation solution that pays a cryptocurrency (TUBE) based on watch time of any type of content. Payouts are taken from a slowly decreasing block reward, calculated based on a user’s AirTime compared to the grand total AirTime per each block. More information can be found at https://bittube.app
