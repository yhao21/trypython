Pinknode Coin Price & Market Data

Pinknode price today is $0.067078536895 with a 24-hour trading volume of $114,422. PNODE price is down -3.8% in the last 24 hours. It has a circulating supply of 77 Million PNODE coins and a total supply of 200 Million. If you are looking to buy or sell Pinknode, Uniswap (v2) is currently the most active exchange.

Pinknode is an "infrastructure-as-a-service" project for Polkadot networks, providing secure, reliable and scalable API endpoints and node operation services to developers and projects.

Pinknode will also be consolidating Polkadot's multitude of parachain nodes under 1 operator for ease of access and simplified solutions, essentially making inter-parachain communication seamless.
