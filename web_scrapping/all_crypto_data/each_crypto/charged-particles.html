Charged Particles Coin Price & Market Data

Charged Particles price today is $0.704539 with a 24-hour trading volume of $61,873. IONX price is down -0.6% in the last 24 hours. It has a circulating supply of 11 Million IONX coins and a total supply of 100 Million. If you are looking to buy or sell Charged Particles, Uniswap (v2) is currently the most active exchange.

Charged Particles are non-custodial NFTs that can be "discharged" at any time by the owner, collecting the interest from the token. And just like any NFT, they are yours trade, transfer, sell, etc. They can also be burned (melted) to reclaim the underlying DAI + interest in full, destroying the token.

Charged Particles are minted with an Asset Token (ie. DAI) and accrue interest via an Interest-Token (ie. CHAI) giving the Token a "Charge". The amount of interest earned from the token represents the "Charge" that the token (Particle) has amassed.
