BGOV Coin Price & Market Data

BGOV price today is $0.014045531131 with a 24-hour trading volume of $31,799. BGOV price is up 13.1% in the last 24 hours. It has a circulating supply of 39 Million BGOV coins and a total supply of 253 Million. If you are looking to buy or sell BGOV, PancakeSwap (v2) is currently the most active exchange.

The BGOV token is an unlimited issuance token within the bZx ecosystem used to govern Binance Smart Chain protocol parameters for Binance specific assets on bZx. bZx protocol is a financial primitive for shorting, leverage, borrowing, and lending that empowers decentralized, efficient, and rent-free blockchain applications.

BGOV can be staked and farmed and earns a portion of BSC fees generated through margin trading, and borrowing on the bZx network. Half of the collected fees on the BSC blockchain are distributed to BGOV stakers. The other half go towards the Insurance Fund.

BGOV is also used to vote on governance proposals for Binance Smart chain protocol-specific assets.
