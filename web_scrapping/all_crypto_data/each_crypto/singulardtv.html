"SingularDTV is laying the foundation for a decentralized entertainment industry. On September 2019, SingularDTV announced the first Censorship Resistant Media DAO: The SNGLS DAO. The SNGLS DAO will provide the non-profit governance layer for the SNGLS Media Distribution Protocol. Established by SingularDTV as an independent and objective organization to guide the development of the protocol, the prime directive of the SNGLS DAO
