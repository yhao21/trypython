Kyber Network Crystal Coin Price & Market Data

Kyber Network Crystal price today is $1.81 with a 24-hour trading volume of $8,272,046. KNC price is up 1.1% in the last 24 hours. It has a circulating supply of 100 Million KNC coins and a total supply of 174 Million. If you are looking to buy or sell Kyber Network Crystal, Binance is currently the most active exchange.
