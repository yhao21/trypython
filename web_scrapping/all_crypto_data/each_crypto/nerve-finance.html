Nerve Finance Coin Price & Market Data

Nerve Finance price today is $0.230791 with a 24-hour trading volume of $608,450. NRV price is up 1.2% in the last 24 hours. It has a circulating supply of 0 NRV coins and a total supply of 78.5 Million. If you are looking to buy or sell Nerve Finance, PancakeSwap (v2) is currently the most active exchange.

Nerve Finance is a stableswap AMM built on Binance Smart Chain designed to allow trading stablecoins and pegged synthetic assets quickly with minimal slippage and low fees. $NRV is the governance token of the Nerve Finance protocol.
