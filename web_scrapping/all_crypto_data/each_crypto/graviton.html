Graviton Coin Price & Market Data

Graviton price today is $4.14 with a 24-hour trading volume of $81,370. GTON price is down -7.9% in the last 24 hours. It has a circulating supply of 3.6 Million GTON coins and a total supply of 21 Million. If you are looking to buy or sell Graviton, Sushiswap is currently the most active exchange.

Graviton is a wrapped tokens’ liquidity incentivization solution that aggregates multiple cross-chain bridges and creates a reward-based economy around asset wrapping.
Graviton provides governance tools for boosting cross-chain liquidity for wrapped digital assets and increasing their yield via GTON reward and governance token. Thereby, Graviton's primary aim is to unite the isolated communities of different blockchain projects like chains, AMMs, farms, and tokens.

Graviton organizes Catalyst, a range of 1-2 weeks-long programs aimed at incentivizing communities of new tokenized projects to boost their initial liquidity on multiple chains.
