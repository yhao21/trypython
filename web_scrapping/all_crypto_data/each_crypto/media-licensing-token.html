Media Licensing Token Coin Price & Market Data

Media Licensing Token price today is $0.251979 with a 24-hour trading volume of $900,310. MLT price is down -5.3% in the last 24 hours. It has a circulating supply of 87 Million MLT coins and a total supply of 200 Million. If you are looking to buy or sell Media Licensing Token, PancakeSwap (v2) is currently the most active exchange.

Incorporated by the german media company Welt der Wunder GmbH, the MILC Platform (Media Industry Licensing Content) aims to provide an open new marketplace for every professional and non-professional content provider and buyer that incorporates broadcast, blockchain and AI-technology.

Platform features include: content licensing, content management, Content marketing and distribution, AI-Subtitling, Governance, Audience Participation, Frame-by-Frame Video player.

It solves four fundamentally important challenges of the global media industry: Global availability, Discovery process for Buyers, Complex licensing and Early feedback from viewers.

The MLT (Media Licensing token) works as payment and contract signing medium, as community rewards vehicle and platform governance token.
