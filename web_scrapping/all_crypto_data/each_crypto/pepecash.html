PepeCash Coin Price & Market Data

PepeCash price today is $0.046125360191 with a 24-hour trading volume of $6,984.18. PEPECASH price is down -14.9% in the last 24 hours. It has a circulating supply of 700 Million PEPECASH coins and a total supply of 1 Billion. If you are looking to buy or sell PepeCash, Dex-Trade is currently the most active exchange.

PepeCash is meant to be the currency of the Pepesphere. About 700 million Pepe Cash are in circulation at the moment. PepeCash is a card just like the other cards on the Rare Pepe directory, except it is a lot more liquid. PepeCash is needed to pay your submission fee to get your Rare Pepes into this directory, and into the wallets. Some people are also working on games that use PepeCash.
