Ubique Chain of Things (UCOT) Coin Price & Market Data

Ubique Chain of Things (UCOT) price today is $0.002150262911 with a 24-hour trading volume of $597.19. UCT price is up 13.1% in the last 24 hours. It has a circulating supply of 520 Million UCT coins and a total supply of 1.05 Billion. If you are looking to buy or sell Ubique Chain of Things (UCOT), Bittrex is currently the most active exchange.

Ubique Chain of Things (UCOT) is a digitized supply chain ecosystem powered by the latest 5G/IoT telecommunications and Blockchain technologies. In UCOT, IoT tracks the commodity flows, while Blockchain guarantees tamperproof data sharing and interoperability across the supply chains. UCOT is designed to ensure transparent and undisputable view of all the actions and transactions by all the parties along the supply chain, achieving ultimate transparency, eliminating discrepancy, inefficiency, and fraud.
