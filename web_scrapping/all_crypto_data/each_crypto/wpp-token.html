Headquartered in Geneva Switzerland, WPP ENERGY GmbH is a leader in the global renewable energy industry since 2009. WPP ENERGY’s mission is to use Innovative and Disruptive Energy and Environmental Technologies to Produce and Provide Affordable and Efficient Clean Energy and has added to its agenda to nearly eliminate garbage landfills globally. PAYMENT TOKEN ON THE GLOBAL GREEN ENERGY FUTURES CONTRACTS BLOCKCHAIN WPP intends to transmit its energy and biofuel production, and that of its partners, into an upcoming Blockchain Based Global Green Energy Futures Contract Trading Platform.
