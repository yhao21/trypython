Yield Guild Games Coin Price & Market Data

Yield Guild Games price today is $7.34 with a 24-hour trading volume of $44,682,886. YGG price is down -1.7% in the last 24 hours. It has a circulating supply of 88 Million YGG coins and a total supply of 1 Billion. If you are looking to buy or sell Yield Guild Games, Sushiswap is currently the most active exchange.

Yield Guild Games (YGG) is a decentralized autonomous organization (DAO) for investing in non fungible tokens (NFTs) used in virtual worlds and blockchain-based games. The organisation’s mission is to create the biggest virtual world economy, optimizing its community-owned assets for maximum utility and sharing its profits with its token holders.
